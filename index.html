About HR Ninja

HR Ninja Consulting is the solution to all your HR
businessneeds. We are a one-stop-shop providing a variety of services. Our focus is on start up companies within a variety of industries. Whether you have a specific HR need or seek relief by outsourcing altogether, we have you covered.

26+ YEARS OF HR EXPEREINCE

Our team is made up of certified and/or trained professionals with over 26+ years of combined HR and Operations experience.
